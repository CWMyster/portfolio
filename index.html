About me

I have been been involved with technology all my life. The Commodore 64 was my first foray into programming as a kid and I think it has helped throughout my life to look for logical solutions to sometimes complex problems. More recently, I try to keep up with the latest technology milestones and try to maintain a larger approach as to where technology is headed. In my spare time, I like to make music and design sound modularly using the German software, Native Instruments Reaktor. I'm also very familiar with a wide variety of digital audio workstations used today. By using many different interfaces and software suites, I think it has honed my skills to approaching solutions efficiently.

Background

I was born and raised in Prior Lake, Minnesota. I attended Hennepin Technical College for Audio Recording, then St. Cloud State university where I pursued a degree in political science. I have traveled as much as possible and would love to travel even more, especially professionally. Recently I enrolled at Hennepin Tech again to learn more about the technology we all value.
